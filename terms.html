Description of the service

BullionView is a personal portfolio tracking application for precious metal collectors. The App allows you to:

  • Record and manage your precious metal holdings (coins and bars)
  • Track the current market value of your collection using live spot prices
  • Monitor portfolio performance and metrics over time
  • Store photographs of your holdings
  • Organise holdings by vault location

BullionView is a personal tracking tool only. It is not a financial services product, investment platform, or dealing service.

Accuracy of financial data

⚠️ Important disclaimer: Spot prices displayed in BullionView are sourced from market data providers and are for informational purposes only. Prices may be delayed, approximate, or differ from actual dealer buy/sell prices. Do not rely on BullionView for trading or financial decisions.

Specifically:

  • Spot prices are refreshed approximately once per hour and may not reflect real-time market conditions
  • Portfolio valuations are estimates based on spot prices and do not account for dealer premiums, spreads, or transaction costs
  • Foreign exchange rates are sourced from the European Central Bank and updated daily
  • Historical portfolio values are reconstructed estimates based on spot prices at time of entry
